Nikku13

Nicholas Potts (Nikku13) is the founder of Dimension XIII. He created this studio so he can keep himself creating and making animations. He has created "Every Day Life" a webcomic that is inspired by Megatokyo, Regular Show, and Edds World. He is currently developing "Infinet Cross", a series that brings all his creations together, holiday specials, and a sketch animated show with his friends.
