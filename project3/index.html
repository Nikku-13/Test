Bio

Nathan Sharp is a musician that is well known for his anime cover songs and voice actor on many anime and video game titles. He is well known on YouTube as NateWantsToBattle and started out uploading parodies of popular songs with video games attached such as Pokemon. Nate has helped create an independent record lable called Give Heart Records and uplaods songs to its YouTube channel.
